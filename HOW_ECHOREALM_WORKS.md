# 🌌 Welcome to EchoRealm: Your Ultimate Virtual Hangout!

Hey there! If you're wondering what **EchoRealm** is all about, you've come to the right place. Imagine a cozy digital clubhouse where you and your friends can hang out, share announcements, whisper secrets, and customize the look of your space. 

No technical jargon here—just a friendly breakdown of how EchoRealm works and how to make the most of it!

---

## 🚀 The Three Main Ways to Talk

In EchoRealm, communication is split into three cool styles depending on who you want to talk to and how loud you want to be:

### 1. 💬 EchoLink (Your Private Corner)
This is your go-to space for direct, personal conversations.
*   **One-on-One Whispers:** Chat privately with any friend. No one else can see your messages.
*   **Group Hangouts:** Create a group chat, add multiple friends, give it a cool name, and upload a custom group profile picture. It's perfect for planning events or just chatting with your inner circle.
*   **Group Admin Powers:** If you create a group chat, you become the **Admin**. Admins have special settings to add new members or kick out existing ones. Regular members only have the option to "Leave Group."
*   **Chat Customization:** Inside any chat, you can click the settings menu (three dots) to:
    *   *Clear Chat:* Wipe the messages from your screen.
    *   *Delete Chatroom:* Remove the chat session entirely.
    *   *Block User:* Instantly block the other person if they are bothering you.
*   **Send Pictures:** Words not enough? You can upload and send images directly inside your chats.

### 2. 📢 EchoShout (The Public Megaphone)
Want everyone in the realm to hear you? Head over to EchoShout!
*   **Public Broadcasts:** Post messages on a global bulletin board that everyone logged into EchoRealm can see in real-time.
*   **Call Out Friends (@Mentions):** Want a specific person to pay attention to your public shout? Type `@` followed by their username to tag them!

### 3. 🤫 EchoMumble (Anonymous Notes)
Sometimes you want to say something without revealing who you are. That’s where EchoMumble comes in!
*   **Anonymous Whispers:** Send a private message (a "Mumble") to another user, and they won't have any idea who sent it.
*   **Safe Space:** You can only send a Mumble to someone if they have explicitly enabled "Accept Mumbles" in their settings. If they want to keep their inbox closed to anonymous messages, they can do that too!

## 👥 Getting Connected (Friends & Account Setup)

EchoRealm is a social network, so connecting with others is super simple:

*   **🔑 Easy Sign-In:** You can register a brand new account using an email, username, and password, or simply click **Continue with Google** to sign in with your Google account instantly.
*   **🔍 Finding People:** Go to the **Find Users** tab, search for usernames, and send them a friend request.
*   **📬 Managing Invitations:** The **Requests** tab shows you all incoming friend requests. You can choose to accept them (making them your friend) or decline them.
*   **👥 Your Friends List:** View all your active connections in the **My Friends** tab.
*   **🛡️ Blocking & Unblocking:** Keep your space safe. If you block someone, they are moved to the **Blocked Users** tab, where you can review or unblock them later.

## 🛠️ Personalize Your Experience

EchoRealm puts you in the driver’s seat when it comes to your profile and how the app looks:

*   **🎭 Control Your Identity:** 
    *   Change your avatar/profile picture whenever you want.
    *   Update your email or username easily.
    *   **Go Anonymous:** Toggle on "Become Anonymous" to hide your real identity details from public posts.
    *   **Mumble Toggle:** Turn "Accept Mumbles" on or off to control whether you want to receive anonymous whispers.
*   **🌓 Day vs. Night Mode:** 
    *   **Light Mode:** A clean, warm cream interface that looks like a beautiful modern design.
    *   **Dark Mode:** A sleek, deep gray interface that's easy on the eyes when you're chatting late at night.
    *   Toggle between them instantly with the moon/sun button in the top right corner (or in your navigation bar)!

---

## 🎨 A Bold, Modern Look (Neo-Brutalism)

You'll notice EchoRealm doesn't look like your typical boring, blurry, or transparent social media apps. It uses a design style called **Neo-Brutalism**:
*   **Hard-edged cards and buttons** that look like physical pieces of paper.
*   **Thick, bold black borders** on everything.
*   **Flat, solid colors** with no annoying gradients or transparency.
*   **Offset black shadows** that give buttons and panels a 3D pop when you hover or click them.
*   It feels playful, retro, and super tactile!

## 💡 Quick Tips for Getting Started

1.  **Check your notifications:** Keep an eye on the little colored badges on your navigation bar—they will tell you when you have new messages, friend requests, or incoming mumbles!
2.  **Use Mobile Nav:** On smaller screens (like phones), all your core tabs (Chats, Shouts, Mumbles, Profile) move to a handy bottom navigation bar for easy access.
